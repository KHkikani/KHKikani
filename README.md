### 👨‍💻 About Me

Highly motivated and results-driven **Senior Flutter Developer** & **Full-Stack Engineer** with **3+ years of professional experience** building and deploying cross-platform mobile & web applications.

- 📱 **Mobile Engineering**: Expert in **Dart & Flutter SDK** across Android, iOS, Web, macOS, and Desktop. Deep hands-on experience with state management (GetX, Provider), MVVM/MVC architectures, and native channels.
- ⚡ **Full-Stack & Backend Systems**: Experienced in designing, building, and self-hosting production backend services using **Node.js**, **Supabase** (PostgreSQL + `pgvector`), **Deno Edge Functions**, **MongoDB**, and **Cloudflare Workers / R2**.
- 🤖 **AI & Vector Embeddings**: Designed end-to-end AI photo recognition workflows with on-device ML Kit, InsightFace / ArcFace 512-dimensional embeddings, and cosine similarity matching.
- 🐳 **DevOps & Infrastructure**: Self-hosting 20+ microservices on VPS using **Docker Compose**, Caddy reverse proxy, Cloudflare CDN/security, and GitHub Actions CI/CD pipelines.
- 👥 **Team Leadership**: Lead developer at Techienutz IT Services, mentoring team members, managing multi-project lifecycles, and communicating with international clients.
